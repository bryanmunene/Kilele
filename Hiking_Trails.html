Explore Our Trails

Popular Trails

Githiga-Karirana

Explore the serene beauty of Githiga Karirana, where gentle hills and tea plantations stretch as far as the eye can see. Walk through lush landscapes, breathe in the fresh air, and enjoy the peaceful charm of this scenic countryside.

Kieni Forest

Discover the tranquility of Kieni Forest, where towering trees and vibrant greenery create a peaceful retreat. Wander through scenic trails, listen to the sounds of wildlife, and experience the calm beauty of this hidden natural gem.

Elephant Hill

Ascend Elephant Hill, where dense forests give way to sweeping views of the Aberdare Range. Hike through bamboo groves and rugged terrain, enjoying the fresh mountain air and breathtaking scenery at every turn.

Table Top Mountain

Explore the majestic Table-Top Mountains, where steep cliffs meet lush valleys. Traverse unique rock formations and witness stunning panoramas. Immerse yourself in the serene beauty of this striking natural wonder.
